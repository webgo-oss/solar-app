..the sun


details

A giant ball of plasma, the Sun provides light and energy essential for life on Earth through nuclear fusion in its core.

Temperature Range

~5,500°C on the surface, ~15 million°C in the core.

Atmosphere

Layers include the photosphere, chromosphere, and corona, composed mainly of hydrogen and helium

Gravity

Surface gravity is about 274 m/s² (28 times Earth’s gravity).

top missions

ISRO: Chandrayaan-3 (2023)

Successfully achieved a soft landing on the Moon's south pole, becoming the first to do so. Demonstrated India's technological prowess in space exploration.

NASA: Perseverance Rover (2021)

Landed on Mars to search for signs of ancient life and collect samples for future return missions. This advanced rover demonstrates cutting-edge scientific exploration.

SpaceX: Falcon Heavy Test Flight (2018)

First successful launch of Falcon Heavy, the most powerful operational rocket, which carried Elon Musk's Tesla Roadster into space, showcasing reusable rocket technology.

Blue Origin: New Shepard Human Flight (2021)

Blue Origin successfully launched its first crewed mission, carrying founder Jeff Bezos and three other passengers, including the oldest and youngest people ever to fly to space.
